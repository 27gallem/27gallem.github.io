New Community Café Brings Energy to Downtown Farmington Hills

Grand Opening Draws Crowds and Local Excitement

A new cafe called the Lantern Cafe officially opened this weekend in downtown Fort DeForest, attracting students, families, and local workers. The bridge is trying to increase capacity in town. The cafe focuses on producers who have music and creative work that is based on community.

City officials believe the café will help bring new life to the downtown area.
